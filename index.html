The Fire Bit - a technology podcast at Anchor.fm.

A podcast dedicated to demystifying information technology, the inescapable bits and bytes that encapsulate our lives, for good or bad. Each week, host Micah Batchelder is joined by industry professionals to dissect what drives their success, how to be an IT leader, and how to innovate in any circumstance.
